How We Protect Your Privacy at The Game Vault

Last Updated:

Your Privacy is Protected

At The Game Vault, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This site is hosted on Google Sites and does not collect, store, or track any personal information. We believe in transparency and want you to understand that your gaming experience is completely private and anonymous.

1 Information We Collect

The Game Vault does not collect, store, or track any personal information from its users. As a Google Sites-hosted platform, we have no access to:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Cookies or tracking mechanisms
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Browsing history or usage analytics
  • IP addresses or device information
  • Any form of personal or behavioral data

You can enjoy all games on The Game Vault completely anonymously without creating an account or providing any information.

2 How We Use Information

Since The Game Vault does not collect any personal information, there is no data for us to use, process, or share.

Our service is designed to:

  • Provide free access to games without requiring any personal information
  • Ensure your gaming experience remains completely private and anonymous
  • Maintain a simple, no-registration gaming platform
  • Protect your privacy by not implementing any tracking or data collection

We have no user data to sell, rent, share, or distribute to anyone.

3 Google Sites and Third-Party Services

The Game Vault is hosted on Google Sites, a platform provided by Google LLC. While we do not collect any personal information:

  • Google Sites: Google may collect standard web server information as part of their hosting service. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on their data practices
  • Third-Party Games: Some games embedded on our site are hosted by third-party providers. These providers may have their own privacy policies and data collection practices
  • External Links: If you click on external links, you will be subject to those websites' privacy policies

We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any information to share. We have no control over third-party game providers or their data practices.

4 Cookies and Tracking

The Game Vault does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. We do not:

  • Set cookies on your browser
  • Use analytics or tracking software
  • Track your browsing behavior
  • Monitor which games you play or how long you play them
  • Use targeted advertising or remarketing technologies
  • Implement any form of user tracking or profiling

However, Google Sites (our hosting platform) and third-party embedded games may use their own cookies. You can control these through your browser settings. We recommend reviewing Google's Privacy Policy for information about their data practices.

5 Children's Privacy

The Game Vault is designed to be safe for users of all ages. We take children's privacy seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • No account registration is required to use our service
  • We do not use targeted advertising or tracking for users of any age
  • Parents and guardians are encouraged to monitor their children's online activities

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

6 Data Security

Since The Game Vault does not collect, store, or process any personal information, there is no user data that requires protection.

Your privacy is inherently protected because:

  • We have no databases storing user information
  • We have no user accounts or profiles
  • We cannot experience data breaches of user information that doesn't exist
  • Your gaming activity is not logged or monitored by us

The security of your browsing experience is managed by Google Sites and your web browser. We recommend keeping your browser updated and following standard internet safety practices.

7 Data Retention

The Game Vault does not retain any user data because we do not collect any user data.

  • No usage analytics or logs are stored
  • No user accounts or profiles exist
  • No browsing history is retained
  • No cookies are set by our site

There is nothing for us to delete or retain. Your visit to The Game Vault leaves no trace of personal information in our systems.

8 Your Rights and Choices

Since The Game Vault does not collect any personal information, you automatically have complete privacy protection:

  • No Data Requests Needed: We have no data about you to provide, delete, or modify
  • Complete Anonymity: You can use our site without any registration or identification
  • Browser Control: You have full control over any cookies set by Google Sites or third-party games through your browser settings
  • Questions Welcome: You can contact us with any privacy-related concerns

Your privacy is protected by default because we operate a zero-data-collection model.
